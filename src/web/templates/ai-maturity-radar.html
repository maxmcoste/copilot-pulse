Skill Gap Index = (L1 + L2) ÷ total population

The proportion of developers who rely solely on passive ghost-text completions (L2) or are effectively inactive (L1). These users represent untapped licence value and are the primary target for upskilling programmes, pair-programming sessions, and AI adoption workshops.

A Skill Gap Index above 60% signals that the majority of the workforce has not yet adopted conversational or agentic AI workflows.

Champion Density = L5 ÷ total population

The share of Elite Agentic developers (L5) in the selected population. These users autonomously orchestrate Copilot through agent mode, CLI pipelines, or achieve more than 30% of their total Copilot activity through agentic actions.

Champions are natural peer-coaches. A Champion Density above 5% gives you enough internal advocates to run meaningful knowledge-transfer programmes without external trainers.
